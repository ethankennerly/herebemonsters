herebemonsters
==============

Game made in 2 days (Ludum Dare December 2010).   http://finegamedesign.com/herebemonsters

HERE BE MONSTERS!
Sail the world and map the monsters

CONTROLS
Arrow key:  Move your ship
After you find the cannon:
X or Space:  Shoot the cannons

HOW TO PLAY
To win:  Map 100% of the world.
To get food:  visit any city or eat a small fish.
To earn gold for your treasure and map:  sail to any city.
If you die:  You return to your visited last city.
You lose your treasure and insurance deducts 5% of your gold.

ADVANCED CONTROLS
Ctrl-1:  Return to normal speed
Ctrl-2:  Too easy?  Try double speed (very hard!)
